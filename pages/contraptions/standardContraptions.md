# Black Market Contraptions

Contraptions are devices to harm hamsters in various ways, but do not net any output other than information.

All contraptions can be manually operated by the player, and most can be automated with an Auto Interactor.

Notably, the **Fire Pit**, **Baking Soda & Vinegar**, and **Trap Cage** cannot be fully automated.

**Baking Soda & Vinegar** accepts an Auto Interactor, but the player must manually supply hamsters.

Contraptions do not provide cash, and only provide information. The amount of information varies greatly bases on stats.
